How to Play Blackjack

Objective

Get as close to 21 as possible without going over. Beat the dealer/other players!

Card Values

  • Number cards (2-10): Face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10 points
  • Ace: 1 or 11 (whichever is better)

Actions

  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand

Game Modes

  • 1v1: 5 rounds, highest score wins
  • 2v2: Team battle, 5 rounds
  • FFA: Up to 5 players, 10 rounds
