How to Play Blackjack

Objective

Get as close to 21 as possible without going over. Beat the dealer's hand.

Card Values

Number cards (2-10) = face value
Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10
Ace = 1 or 11 (your choice)

Actions

Hit: Take another card
Stand: Keep your current hand
Double: Double your bet and take one more card

Winning

Beat the dealer's hand or have the dealer bust (go over 21). Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card) pays 3:2.
